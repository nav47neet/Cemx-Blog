Meet cemX The Unique Startup In The Cement Industry

India is fast becoming a major global marketplace and very soon a developed country due to arduous contributions from entrepreneurs and evolving neophyte startups playing crucial roles. Comprehending the situation, startups are striving towards excellence through innovations that can make positive changes in the lives of the common populace.

cemX is one unique startup that has been recognized by Startup India at a very early stage due to its broad vision of adding values and ease of accessibility to the lives of common people across the country. Understanding the adaptability pace, and how the general consumer market is switching to an online world of digital platforms, cemX recognized it appositely and have effectively adapted the same.

cemX is India’s first online platform that is in sync with the offline segment extension where individuals can buy different brands of cement at the cheapest price when compared to the local dealers. Individuals either can order directly on its dedicated platform or through local cemX e-dealers. At cemX, one can order in a minute and get their order delivered at their doorsteps in a day’s time guaranteeing the best price as compared to the local market markup.

Mr Ashutosh Kumar (Jha), the founder & CEO of cemX says “Being a citizen everyone deserves their dream home, we at cemX are trying to turn the dreams of common people into reality. We are contributing our best to support the digital India initiative by making cement deals hassle-free and easily available on the door at the best competitive price.”

Ashutosh met Mr Sourav Chakraborty, his senior from graduate school, in Kolkata and shared his vision to welcome him on board as a co-founder and CBO. Mr Chakraborty feels “Modern-day businesses exist on their disruptive potential. We at cemX want to break the age-old orthodox walls of the Indian cement Industry only to build newer houses for the New Smart India.”

Both the founders have travelled the world to learn and work, and now they want to contribute back to India in building a New India by effectively applying their shares of knowledge.

The Board is constantly on the lookout to attract the best minds into cemX with a common goal of revolutionising the cement marketplace. At cemX people are from some of the best universities in the world like Harvard University, Boston University, Imperial College London, IIT, etc.

Looking at the experience shared by one of our research Intern (Mr Hariom, class of 2021 IIT-Delhi): “My internship experience has taught me more than I ever expected. This internship enhanced my professional communication skills, Networking and time management. cemX has given me a better opportunity to show my potential and a better understanding of my skill. During the summer internship in cemX, I was always keen and inquisitive to explore the business approaches in accordance with the need of the market. Business acumen and market research were shared by my mentor Suyash Ram Pandey which was a worthy learning experience. I want to thank cemX team and Ashutosh Sir for giving me this break.”

About Us

cemX is India's first online platform where a customer can buy cement of different brands available in the Indian market at the best competitive price. It is an e-commerce aggregator platform that efficiently redesigned the structure of an orthodox cement distribution channel in India thereby democratizing the purchasing power of the consumers namely the middle and lower class segments of Indian society. Registered and certified by Startup India, cemX empowers the Government of India’s ‘Digital India’ initiative. Anybody (small businesses or individuals) can order directly through our online platform or can visit the nearest e-dealers (our offline e-stores) for assistance.
